The Heyer BV30ST Pro is built for patients who need a higher level of ventilatory assistance than standard CPAP therapy. It offers multiple clinical modes and controlled pressure delivery for home and clinic use.

## Key Highlights
- Multiple ventilation modes including CPAP, Auto CPAP, S, Auto S, T, S/T, and VGPS support.
- Intelligent pressure control for more stable respiratory support.
- Suitable for more advanced therapy planning in both home and clinical settings.

## Ideal For
- Patients with more complex sleep-disordered breathing needs.
- Cases requiring spontaneous or timed support modes.
- Providers looking for broader ventilation flexibility in one device.
