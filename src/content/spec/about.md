# About Tadawi Life Tech

Tadawi Life Tech is a medical technology company based in Muscat, Oman, dedicated to improving sleep quality, respiratory health, and overall quality of life.

Our focus is on delivering clinically reliable, patient-centered solutions that support the management of sleep-related breathing disorders, respiratory conditions, and patient mobility needs.

> Quality sleep is the foundation of a healthy life.  
> Your Life... Our Commitment.

## What We Do

With a strong specialization in sleep apnea and sleep-related breathing disorders, Tadawi has positioned itself as a focused provider in this field within the Omani market.

In sleep health, we offer a comprehensive range of solutions for the management of sleep apnea and snoring, including:

- Sleep study services
- CPAP and BiPAP therapy devices
- Related sleep technologies designed to improve sleep quality

In respiratory care, we provide solutions that support breathing and help manage conditions such as sinusitis and allergic rhinitis, including:

- Oxygen therapy systems
- Nebulizers
- Nasal irrigation devices

Tadawi also offers a range of orthopedic braces and supports designed to enhance mobility, support recovery, and improve patient comfort.

## Beyond Equipment Supply

Our role goes beyond supplying medical equipment. We support the full care pathway through:

- Product consultation
- Device setup
- Patient education
- Ongoing support

This approach helps ensure a reliable, practical, and patient-focused experience.

By combining internationally recognized technologies with a deep understanding of the local healthcare landscape, Tadawi aims to be a trusted partner for healthcare providers and patients across Oman.

## Mission

To improve sleep quality, respiratory health, and patient mobility by delivering specialized, clinically grounded solutions.

- To provide advanced and up-to-date medical solutions and equipment in the fields of sleep disorders, respiratory conditions, and orthopedic support, based on scientific evidence, clinical validation, and the highest international standards.
- To support patients and healthcare professionals through consultation, training, and continuous support.
- To offer 24/7 support services that help ensure proper use of medical equipment and provide peace of mind for patients.
- To deliver practical, patient-centered solutions tailored to the needs of individuals with sleep disorders, respiratory conditions, and orthopedic requirements.
- To collaborate with healthcare professionals and medical institutions to enhance the quality of healthcare services in Oman.
- To bridge the gap between advanced medical technologies and the real needs of the local healthcare market.

## Vision

To be recognized as a trusted reference in providing advanced and accessible solutions for sleep apnea treatment and other specialized medical equipment, with the aim of improving the quality of patient care in Oman and the wider region.
