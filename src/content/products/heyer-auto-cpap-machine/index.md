The Heyer 20A Pro is designed for patients who need stable, comfortable airway support at home. It combines automatic pressure response, ramp support, and humidification to improve adherence and reduce treatment discomfort.

## Key Highlights
- Automatic pressure adjustment that responds to breathing patterns throughout the night.
- AS-ELEX pressure relief and auto-ramp support for a more comfortable sleep transition.
- Integrated humidification and heated tubing support to reduce dryness and improve comfort.

## Ideal For
- Patients prescribed CPAP or APAP therapy for obstructive sleep apnea.
- Users who want a more comfortable setup with ramp and exhalation relief.
- Home therapy programs that need dependable nightly use.
