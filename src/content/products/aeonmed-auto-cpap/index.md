The Aeonmed AS100A is designed to deliver dependable CPAP therapy with a patient-friendly interface and comfort-focused features. It supports pressure automation, humidity control, and easier acclimatization at bedtime.

## Key Highlights
- Automatic pressure adjustment based on overnight breathing behavior.
- Pressure relief and auto-ramp features to improve comfort at sleep onset.
- Integrated humidifier and mask-fit support for a smoother therapy setup.

## Ideal For
- Users starting home therapy for obstructive sleep apnea.
- Patients who want an auto-adjusting CPAP platform.
- Setups that need humidification and comfort-focused features in one device.
