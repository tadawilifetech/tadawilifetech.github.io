This fleece hose cover adds an insulating layer around standard PAP tubing to make therapy quieter and more comfortable. It also helps reduce the rainout effect caused by temperature differences inside the hose.

## Key Highlights
- Helps reduce condensation inside the tubing.
- Adds a softer surface where the hose touches the skin or bedding.
- Can reduce disturbance from hose movement and noise.

## Ideal For
- Users with humidified PAP therapy.
- People sensitive to cold tubing against the skin.
- Sleep setups that need better hose comfort and control.
