Oniris is a clinically recognized mandibular advancement device designed to reduce upper-airway collapse during sleep. By moving the lower jaw slightly forward, it helps maintain airflow and supports quieter, more stable sleep.

## Key Highlights
- Non-invasive oral therapy option for snoring and selected OSA cases.
- Supports airway patency by advancing the lower jaw during sleep.
- Includes brochure and clinical reference documents for deeper review.

## Ideal For
- Adults with snoring or mild to moderate obstructive sleep apnea.
- Patients seeking an alternative to conventional mask-based therapy.
- Clinician-guided treatment pathways that include oral sleep devices.
