Choosing the right mask is one of the most important parts of successful PAP therapy. This product category helps patients find a fit that balances seal quality, comfort, and freedom of movement.

## Key Highlights
- Includes options for full-face, nasal, and nasal pillow therapy styles.
- Designed to reduce leakage and improve stability during sleep.
- Supports better comfort and adherence by matching the mask to the user’s breathing pattern.

## Ideal For
- Users selecting a first CPAP mask.
- Patients who want to change mask style for better comfort.
- Home therapy setups that need the right balance of seal and movement.
